# Support

Welcome to VilChain! If you need assistance or have questions, we're here to help. Below are various ways you can seek support:

## Documentation

Check out our [official documentation](docs/) for detailed information on setting up, configuring, and using VilChain.

## Issue Tracker

If you encounter a bug, have a feature request, or need to report an issue, please visit our [GitHub Issue Tracker](https://github.com/your-username/VilChain/issues) and create a new issue. Make sure to provide as much detail as possible to help us understand and address your concern.

## Community Forum

Join our community on the [VilChain Forum](https://community.vilchain.com) to connect with other users, share experiences, and seek advice. This is a great place for discussions and community support.

## Stack Overflow

For specific programming-related questions or technical issues, you can use [Stack Overflow](https://stackoverflow.com) with the tag `vilchain`. Our community and developers actively monitor this tag to provide assistance.

## Chat

For real-time discussions and quick questions, you can join our community chat on [Gitter](https://gitter.im/VilChain/community).

## Email Support

If you prefer email communication, you can reach out to our support team at support@vilchain.com. Please allow some time for a response, as our team may be handling multiple inquiries.

## Contributing

If you're interested in contributing to VilChain, check out our [Contribution Guidelines](CONTRIBUTING.md) for information on how to get involved.

We appreciate your interest in VilChain and hope you have a positive experience using our project!

Happy coding!
